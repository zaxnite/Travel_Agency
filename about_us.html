Get To Know Us

Our Story

Since 2023, Escape Artists has always had its customers as its upmost priority. Dedicated in provided a personalized international travel service for everyone. Whether you're looking to explore exotic new destinations, embark on a thrilling adventure, or simply relax and unwind in paradise, our team of Escape Artists is here to make it happen.

So why wait? Let Escape Artists be your guide to the world of travel and embark on your next great adventure today!

Company Profile

Escape Artists is owned by a team of five passionate and experienced professionals who share a love for adventure and a dedication to providing exceptional travel services. Each of our owners brings a unique set of skills and experiences to the table, allowing us to offer a diverse range of travel packages and services that cater to the unique needs and preferences of our clients.

Our Journey

Our journey began in a coffee shop between a group of friends who shared a common passion for travel. With a shared vision and a desire to create a travel agency that offered something different, we decided to pool our resources and make it a reality. Our friendship and shared values continue to drive us forward as we strive to provide exceptional travel experiences to our clients.
